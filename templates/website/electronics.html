Electronics

The term "Electronics" refers to the study of the flow of electrons in electrical circuits. In Electronics, we learn how an electron behaves under various conditions of external fields, a technique called electron mechanics. Electronics can also be defined as the branch of engineering concerned with the study and application of electronic devices.The device that controls the flow of electrons is called an electronic device. The main building block of an electronic circuit is an electronic device.
